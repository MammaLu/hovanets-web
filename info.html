You can save your Tree. Even if you are just out of the shell!

Point-and-click adventure based on Carpathian fairy tales for PC

  • explore and restore different locations and secret places to find and collect quest items
  • solve puzzles
  • communicate with NPCs for receiving quests, items, tips and tricks
  • find, restore and collect the oven tiles
  • write your own stories about the Tree House and its inhabitants

A newborn Hovanets - a kind of house-elf in Carpathian mythology - found himself alone in an abandoned house inside a giant dying tree. What kind of disaster happened to the tree? Why the inhabitants gone? And what the role of the house-elf in the story? Explore, keep the tree alive, and uncover the secrets of the place.
